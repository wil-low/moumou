## **1. Deck & Initial Deal**

* **Deck:** 36 cards, ranks `6 7 8 9 T J Q K A` in suits `s h d c`.
* **Card notation:** `Rs` (rank + suit), e.g., `Th`, `Js`.
* **Initial deal:**

  * Each player receives **5 cards**.
  * The dealer places **their last dealt card** on the table as the **first card played**.
  * Dealer may then continue with **covering or combo plays** if applicable.
* **Table visibility:** All cards played are tracked; only the **last 4 cards** are ‚Äúvisible‚Äù for strategy purposes.

## **2. Turn Mechanics**

* On a player‚Äôs turn, they may play:

  * A card matching the **last card‚Äôs rank** or **suit**, or
  * A **Jack** (always playable).
* **Combos:** Multiple cards of the same rank may be played in a single turn.
* **6:** Can be played after any card. After playing a 6, you must **cover** it by playing:

  * Another 6,
  * A card of the same suit, or
  * Any Jack.
  * If none, draw cards **one by one until a valid cover is found**.
* If a player cannot play:

  * Draw **one card at a time** until playable.
  * If still no playable card ‚Üí skip turn.

## **3. Special Card Effects**

## **4. Moumou Event**

* If **last 4 visible cards** are all of the **same rank**, a **Moumou event** occurs.
* Game ends immediately, proceed with scoring.

---

## **5. Deck Depletion**

* If deck is empty, take **all table cards except the last 4**, shuffle, and form a new deck.

---

## **6. Scoring**

* At game end (either by empty hand or Moumou):

  * **Jacks:** 20 pts
  * **Aces:** 15 pts
  * **Kings/Queens:** 10 pts
  * **Number cards:** face value (6‚Äì10)
* **Cumulative match:**

  * Players start at 0 pts.
  * After each game, points are added.
  * If a player **exceeds agreed point limit**, they **lose the match**.

---

## **7. Endgame Restrictions**

* A player **cannot win** by playing:

  * A **6** (must cover first)
  * An **Ace** (opponent draws 1; if unplayable ‚Üí skip turn)

---

## **8. Strategy Tips**

1. **Hand Management**

   * Avoid leaving Jacks, Aces, or 6s as last cards unless you can cover or use points advantage.
   * Dump high-value cards early if safe.
   * Keep flexible cards for combos or covering.

2. **Offense**

   * Play 7‚Äôs, 8‚Äôs, and Kc to **force opponent draws or skip turns**.
   * Use Jacks to demand suits and control flow.

3. **Endgame**

   * Plan moves to empty your hand **without leaving a 6 or Ace** as last card.
   * Last-card Jacks: strategically add points to opponent or subtract from self.
   * Monitor for **Moumou risk** in last 4 table cards.

4. **Suit & Combo Control**

   * Chain combos to shed multiple cards quickly.
   * Keep diverse suits to maximize playable options.

5. **Match Context**

   * Ahead in points: play defensively, minimize high-card penalties.
   * Behind: use Jacks, force Moumou events, offload high-value cards.
   * Balance short-term moves with long-term scoring.
